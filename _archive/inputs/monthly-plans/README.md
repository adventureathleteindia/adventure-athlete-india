# Monthly Plans Input

Drop any of these here and ask Claude to process them:

## Accepted Formats
- **Voice notes** (.m4a, .mp3, .wav) - record your plans while riding/walking
- **Text notes** (.txt, .md) - quick typed notes
- **Screenshots** (.png, .jpg) - calendar screenshots, race entries, etc.
- **PDFs** - race registrations, event details, travel bookings

## What to Include
Just talk naturally about your month:
- Training blocks (heavy week, recovery week, etc.)
- Planned adventures (treks, big rides, new routes)
- Races or events
- Tours booked or expected
- Travel plans
- Rest days / off days
- Any special occasions

## Example Voice Note
> "January - first two weeks I'm doing base training, nothing crazy.
> 15th is Hatu Peak trek with two guests.
> Third week easy, maybe some city rides.
> 28th I'm going to Manali for that trail race.
> End of month recovery."

## After Dropping Files
Just tell Claude: "Process my January plans" or "Check my new inputs"
