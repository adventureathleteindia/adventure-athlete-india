# Brainstorming Session - Adventure Athlete India

**Date:** 2025-12-29
**Participants:** Client (Adventure Athlete) + BA Agent

---

## Questions & Answers Summary

### Q1: Current follower count?
- Instagram: 1,200
- YouTube: 1,100
- Facebook: 500

### Q2: How often do you post?
- Rarely (1-2 times/month) or occasionally (1-2 times/week)

### Q3: Content type?
- Mix of photos and short videos
- Heavy on stories

### Q4: Biggest struggle with posting?
- No proper sequence of posts
- Don't know what audience wants
- Normal content goes viral, extreme stuff doesn't

### Q5: Location and adventure radius?
- Shimla city, 50km radius
- Can go out every weekend or at least biweekly

### Q6: Competitive background?
- 8 years MTB
- 6 years trail running
- 5 National Championships
- 20+ races with international participants
- ITRA score ~555
- 10+ trail races with podiums
- Raced at 4300m altitude
- Can dedicate 1 hour daily

### Q7: Ideal customer?
- Adventure tourists (foreigners/city folks) wanting unique Himalayan experience
- Partner with local hotels
- Later: serious athletes for training camps

### Q8: Existing setup?
- Starting from scratch
- Has old Strava routes to import
- Needs proper shoot script for adventure days

### Q9: Tour experiences priority?
- All 4 types:
  1. Explore Shimla with Me (half/full day)
  2. Weekend Trail Experience (1-2 days)
  3. Peak Expedition (1-2 days, fast hikes)
  4. High Altitude Training Camp (7-14 days, Phase 2)
- Price range: ₹10-15K/day

### Q10: Booking experience?
- Hybrid approach (Option D)
- Browse → Questionnaire → Custom quote

### Q11: Route documentation vision?
- 3 photos for short routes
- 6-10 photos for epic routes
- GPX from Strava
- Vlog only for epic/famous routes
- Quality over quantity

### Q12: 12-month goals?
- Instagram: 10K+
- YouTube: 10K+
- Tours: 2 per month
- Routes documented: 2 epic per month
- Focus on GROWTH, not leads initially

### Q13: Equipment?
- Has everything: smartphone, GoPro, drone, gimbal, editing apps, laptop

### Q14: Language preference?
- English for website (international tourists)
- Hindi for YouTube vlogs (better engagement)

### Q15: Daily routine?
- Morning workout: 8-10 AM
- Work: 11 AM - 7 PM
- Editing time: 9-10 PM
- Weekends: Full day alternate, half day others
- Don't want to overburden

---

## Key Insights from Session

1. **Underselling credentials** - 5x National Champion is huge but not leveraged
2. **Consistency is the issue** - Not content quality
3. **Stories connect, stunts impress** - Need more storytelling
4. **Morning workout = content capture** - Already has the time built in
5. **Premium positioning is right** - Has the credentials to charge ₹10-15K/day
6. **Sustainable > Ambitious** - 3 posts/week is the sweet spot

---

## Decisions Made

1. Tagline: "Ride. Run. Explore. Himalayas."
2. Website: 7 pages (Home, About, Experiences, Routes, Corporate Wellness, Book, Contact)
3. Corporate Wellness: Partner programs, visually distinct
4. Content: 3 posts/week (2 Reels + 1 Carousel)
5. Language: Hindi YouTube, English website
6. Routes: 3 photos short, 6-10 epic, vlog for epic only
7. Booking: Questionnaire → Custom quote flow
