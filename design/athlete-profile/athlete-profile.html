Profile

About Atul

Atul Chauhan is an engineer turned competitive trail runner and mountain biker based in Shimla, Himachal Pradesh — the heart of the Indian Himalayas. Since his debut at Hero MTB Himalaya in 2016, where he finished 10th in the open men's solo category, he has built a decade-long competitive career that spans mountain biking nationals, trail ultras, vertical kilometre races, and duathlons.

A 3x HP State Team member at Mountain Biking Nationals, multiple race winner (Tuffman 10K 2022 & 2024, HASTPA Duathlon 2023), and consistent podium finisher across disciplines, Atul brings a rare versatility to Indian endurance sport — equally competitive on a bike and on foot.

Beyond racing, Atul is the founder of Adventure Athlete India — a licensed Himalayan adventure guide service offering curated trail running, mountain biking, hiking experiences, and multi-day tours for domestic and international clients.

He holds a Licensed HP Tourism Guide certification (Reg: 080724 42383, valid through August 2027) and is one of a select few athletes in India who compete at the state and national level while simultaneously operating an adventure tourism business.

With a B.Tech in Computer Science and an MBA, Atul brings an engineer's mindset to everything he does — combining technical discipline with athletic grit and business acumen to build Adventure Athlete India into a growing platform that connects athletes and adventure seekers with the raw, unfiltered Himalayas.
