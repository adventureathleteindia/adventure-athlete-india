FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your adventure.

Booking

How do I book a tour?

Fill the "Plan Your Adventure" form. I'll reach out within 24-48 hours to discuss and customize your experience.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 3-5 days for day trips. 1-2 weeks for multi-day adventures.

Is this a private tour?

Yes, all tours are private - just you/your group and me. No strangers.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, Bank Transfer, or Cash. 50% advance to confirm, 50% before tour.

What's included in the price?

Guide fee only. Extras (transport, bike rental, food) depend on your choices. You can pay providers directly, or I maintain a record and you settle with me at the end.

Cancellation

What's the cancellation policy?

Inform me in advance = full refund. Flexible for genuine cases. Just don't ghost!

What if weather is bad?

If I cancel due to weather, you get full refund or reschedule option.

Safety

Do you provide insurance?

No. I recommend carrying personal insurance that covers adventure activities.

What if I get injured?

I will provide immediate assistance and help you reach appropriate medical care.

Do I need to be very fit?

Depends on the activity. I'll recommend appropriate difficulty based on your fitness.

Gear

Do I need my own bike/gear?

Your choice: bring your own or rent locally. I'll send a gear checklist and can sort out rentals for you if needed.

What should I bring?

Varies by tour. I'll send a checklist after booking.

Experience

Can beginners join?

Yes! I have easy nature walks to expert MTB trails. We'll match your level.

Can family members come as support?

Yes, with prior arrangement and minimal/no extra fee.

Logistics

Where do tours start?

Usually Shimla area, sometimes nearby towns. Exact GPS location shared before tour. Pickup can be arranged.

Should I arrive early?

Recommended 1-3 days before for rest and acclimatization, especially for high-altitude adventures.

Still have questions?

I'm happy to chat and answer anything else.
